What is PCOS and PCOD?

Understanding PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)

PCOS is a hormonal disorder causing ovarian cysts, irregular periods, and androgen overproduction, affecting overall health.

What About PCOD (Polycystic Ovarian Disease)?

PCOD results in enlarged ovaries due to immature egg release. It shares symptoms with PCOS but is generally less severe.
